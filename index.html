05 - FAQS

We got you an answer

Everything you might want to know before we work together - answered clearly and simply

What kind of projects do you take on?

I focus on product design, UX strategy, and interface design for digital products - mainly web and mobile. I work with startups, scal-ups, and established brands who value thoughtful, user-centered design.

Do you offer freelance or consulting services?

Yes, I offer both freelance and consulting services depending on the project scope and timeline. Whether you need end-to-end product design or a strategic UX audit, I'm happy to collaborate.

Can we work remotely?

Absolutely. I regularly collaborate with clients from all over the world. Communication is seamless through tools like Zoom, Slack, and Notion.

What’s your typical design process?

I typically follow a user-centered design process that includes discovery, wireframing, prototyping, user testing, and final UI design. Each phase is adapted to suit your project's needs.

Do you also handle development?

While my core expertise lies in design, I collaborate with skilled developers or your in-house team to ensure smooth handoff and implementation. I can also support with front-end guidance and design QA.
